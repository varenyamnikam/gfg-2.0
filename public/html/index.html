What's GFG X PCCOER ?

GeeksforGeeks Student Chapters are university-based community chapters creating a coding culture for students interested in Computer Science and other core technical competencies.


Perks
  1. Get an internship at GeeksforGeeks
  2. Grow their knowledge in a peer-to-peer learning environment
  3. Learn to build effective and optimized solutions for local businesses and their community as per the latest technology trends
  4. Great learning and networking experience

What can we help you find?

GeeksforGeeks student Chapters will provide students with various events and webinars on coding or placements. Students may get internship opportunities at GeeksforGeeks. Along with all these students can get exclusive discounts on GFG courses. You will get an opportunity to participate in competition on a PAN India level. It will help students to grow a network among 145+ colleges.

We will be having Coding Events through GFG IDE. We will have webinar sessions through GFG. We will be organizing a webinar with someone from your network who is good at the domain in which students are interested in. Various Fun Quizes,competitions etc. Rest, It is open to creative imagination.
